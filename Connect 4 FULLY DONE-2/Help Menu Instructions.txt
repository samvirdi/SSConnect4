Use the mouse to navigate throughout the program, to play the game you just have to click the column you want the chip to go and it will appear there. To check the high scores press back and click the high score menu.
The objective of the game is to connect four of one's own discs of the same color next to each other vertically, horizontally, or diagonally before your opponent.
